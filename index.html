1. Generic vs Personal Email Performance

Comparison of reply rates between generic company emails (info@, frontdesk@, manager@, etc.) and personal work emails (firstname@company.com). Generic emails make up 12% of all leads contacted but 33% of qualified positives.

Email TypeContactedRepliedReply RatePositivePositive Reply Rate
Generic Emails859394.5%1538.5%
Personal Work Emails6,0632123.5%3014.2%
Key Insight: Generic emails reply at a higher rate (4.5% vs 3.5%) and convert replies to positives at nearly 2.7x the rate (38.5% vs 14.2%). While personal emails generated more total positives (30 vs 15) due to volume, generic emails are significantly more efficient — likely because the person managing the inbox (often an owner/manager at smaller businesses) is the decision-maker.
